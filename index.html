Fieldwork & Action

Leading by Example

As part of my Welsh Bacc project, I spent time litter-picking at Rogerstone station. This hands-on experience allowed me to categorize the types of waste found in high-traffic commuter areas and analyze the local environmental impact.

  • Location: Rogerstone station (Afon)
  • Primary Findings: High volume of single-use plastics and smoking-related waste.
  • Outcome: 2 full bags of litter removed from the platform surroundings.
